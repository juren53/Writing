# Mr Truman's Music - Preface

**Preface**

  


 **Mr Trunan's Music:**

 **how music shaped the man who would become the 33rd president**

  


There are as many reasons to write a book and this one is no different. I felt there is a story to tell. About a boy, a man. a President, a citizen and how a life long passion helped mold that person.

  


Many books have been written about Harry S. Truman. Each author strives to describe our 33rd in a new and unique light. This book will tell the story of the role music played in his life and how it helped shape the man that became our 33rd president.

  


Like many musicians, Harry was his own worst critic.
