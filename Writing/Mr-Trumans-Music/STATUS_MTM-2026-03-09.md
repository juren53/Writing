## Key Research Leads (Priority Order)

1. **HSTL correspondence** — Truman's letters to Bess and diary entries mentioning music; the Daniel Pollock correspondence (10 years of letters)
2. **Daniel Pollock** — pianist Truman befriended; Truman recommended him for a Fulbright to Vienna; played presidential favorites before joint session of Congress at the Centennial. Likely well-documented at HSTL.
3. **Specific Chopin and Mozart pieces** — Margaret says he played these from memory in old age. Identify the pieces; tie back to Mrs. White's curriculum.
4. **"The President's Pianist"** — a book on this topic appears in `Sources/Photos/` (screenshot). Identify as potential source or comp title.
5. **Blue Danube connection** — Pollock replaced Truman's worn-out *Blue Danube* recording. Cross-ref HSTR 513 (*Strauss Waltzes*, Decca) in the CSV.
6. **Unidentified photos** — ~12 photos with only HSTL catalog numbers need identification and captioning.
7. **Lineage ODT and Recital Program PDF** — not yet read; likely contain detail for Ch. 3.
